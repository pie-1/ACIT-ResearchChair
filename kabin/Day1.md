## Key Takeaways
- Modern web apps avoid full page reloads — JS fetches data and updates the DOM directly
- SPAs feel faster because only data moves, not entire pages
- The browser-server communication cycle is the foundation everything else builds on
- Full Stack Open's pace is fast — reading carefully matters more than rushing parts
